How to take care of a dog - Beginners

Here one of our workers, Adam Teo teaching the basics of taking care of a dog.

  1. Nutrition & Hydration – Provide a balanced diet with high-quality dog food and ensure fresh water is always available.

  2. Exercise & Mental Stimulation – Daily walks, playtime, and engaging activities help maintain physical and mental well-being.

  3. Grooming & Hygiene – Regular brushing, nail trimming, and ear cleaning keep your dog comfortable and healthy.

  4. Veterinary Care – Routine check-ups, vaccinations, and parasite prevention are essential for long-term health.

  5. Training & Socialization – Teaching basic commands and exposing dogs to different environments ensures good behavior and confidence.

  6. Dental Care – Regular brushing and dental treats prevent gum disease and bad breath.

  7. Love & Affection – Dogs thrive on companionship and need a loving, stimulating environment.

  8. >By following these steps, you can ensure your furry friend leads a happy and healthy life.
